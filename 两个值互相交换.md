### 两个值互相交换
 >用异或运算：一个值加1再与1异或运算后再减去1
```sql
SELECT id, (id+1)^1-1, student FROM seat
```
| id | (id+1)^1-1 | student |
|----|------------|---------|
| 1  | 2          | Abbot   |
| 2  | 1          | Doris   |
| 3  | 4          | Emerson |
| 4  | 3          | Green   |
| 5  | 6          | Jeames  |
